# Available Datasets

This document provides a comprehensive list of datasets available in AgriFoodPy Data, organized by module. Each dataset includes a brief description, source, and usage notes.

## Food

- **FAOSTAT**: Food Balance Sheets data from FAO, including production, trade, and consumption statistics.
- **Nutrients_FAOSTAT**: Nutrient composition data for various food items from FAOSTAT.

## Impact

- **PN18**: Environmental impact data from Poore & Nemecek (2018) study on food systems.
- **PN18_FAOSTAT**: Merged PN18 and FAOSTAT data for comprehensive impact analysis.
- **PN18_regional**: Regional breakdowns of PN18 impact data.
- **UKNDC_FAOSTAT**: UK-specific nutrient and dietary composition data merged with FAOSTAT.

## Land

- **NaturalEngland_ALC_1000**: Agricultural Land Classification at 1km resolution from Natural England.
- **NaturalEngland_ALC_2000**: Agricultural Land Classification at 2km resolution from Natural England.
- **NaturalEngland_ALC_5000**: Agricultural Land Classification at 5km resolution from Natural England.
- **UKCEH_LC_1000**: Land Cover data at 1km resolution from UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology.
- **UKCEH_LC_1000_gb**: Great Britain subset of UKCEH Land Cover at 1km.
- **UKCEH_LC_1000_ni**: Northern Ireland subset of UKCEH Land Cover at 1km.
- **UKCEH_LCPCP_1000_gb**: Land Cover Parcel Crop Presence at 1km for Great Britain.
- **UKCEH_LCPCP_2000_gb**: Land Cover Parcel Crop Presence at 2km for Great Britain.
- **UKCEH_LCPCP_5000_gb**: Land Cover Parcel Crop Presence at 5km for Great Britain.

## Population

- **Population_FAOSTAT**: Population data from FAOSTAT.
- **UN**: Population data from United Nations.
- **UK_ONS**: Population data from UK Office for National Statistics.
